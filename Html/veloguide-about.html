About VeloGuide

VeloGuide is a urban bike guide desgined to help you find safe and or posted bike routes around many of the urban bike friendly cities of america. We currently offer bike maps, bike parking, quick lookup law and guides for the citeis and metro areas of Chicago, New York, San Fransico and Portland.

To find out more go to the VeloGuide web site and take a look around, and if you know of a government sponsored or available private map we should add for your city let us know, or ask us to build a map or custom VeloGuide application for your city or business.
